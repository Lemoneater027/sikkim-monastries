About Sikkim Buddhism

Sikkim, nestled in the Eastern Himalayas, is a sacred land where Buddhism has flourished for centuries. The state is home to numerous ancient monasteries that serve as centers of spiritual learning, cultural preservation, and architectural marvel.

These monasteries, belonging primarily to the Nyingma and Kagyu sects of Tibetan Buddhism, offer visitors a glimpse into the rich spiritual heritage of the region. Each monastery tells a unique story of devotion, artistic excellence, and historical significance.
