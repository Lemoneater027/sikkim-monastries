Getting to Sikkim

By Air: Bagdogra Airport (124km) - 4 hours to Gangtok
By Rail: New Jalpaiguri (NJP) - 125km, well connected
By Road: NH10 connects via Siliguri, regular bus services
üìÑ

Permits & Documentation

Indian Nationals: No permits required (except North Sikkim)
Foreign Tourists: RAP (Restricted Area Permit) required
North Sikkim: Special PAP permit through travel agencies
üå§Ô∏è

Best Time to Visit

Peak Season: March-June (Spring/Early Summer)
Good Season: September-December (Autumn/Winter)
Avoid: July-August (Monsoon rains)

Essential Tips

  • Dress modestly - cover shoulders and legs
  • Remove shoes before entering prayer halls
  • Ask permission before photography
  • Maintain silence and respectful behavior
  • Carry cash for donations and remote areas
  • Book accommodations in advance during peak season
